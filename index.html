About Us

SafeHouse is a comprehensive facility management application designed for facility owners and managers. It is versatile enough to be used across various types of facilities, including rental properties, commercial spaces, and other facilities requiring maintenance, security, and asset management.

The application is structured into four core modules:

  • Maintenance Management
  • Asset Management
  • Access Control
  • Client Experience

Safehouse supports centralized management of single or multiple facilities and is accessible via both web and mobile applications, each tailored to meet specific operational needs.

Safehouse Modules

Maintenance Management Module

Our Maintenance Management module ensures seamless management of all maintenance tasks. Key features include:

  • Automated Work Order Creation
  • Service Tracking
  • Real-time Reporting

Streamline maintenance processes, enhance responsiveness, and keep facilities in optimal condition.

Asset Management Module

The Asset Management module provides complete oversight of facility assets. Key features include:

  • Inventory Tracking
  • Lifecycle Management
  • Performance Analysis

Optimize asset utilization, reduce costs, and extend asset lifecycles with this comprehensive module.

Access Control Module

The Access Control module is designed to enhance security across facilities. Key features include:

  • Automated Access Requests
  • Visitor Management
  • Real-time Monitoring

The system automates access requests for residents and employees, and offers secure management of visitor access. Real-time monitoring ensures compliance with security protocols and improves overall safety.

Client Experience Module

The Client Experience module improves tenant interactions by providing:

  • Online Payment Options
  • Service Requests
  • Tenant Feedback

This module enables residents to pay rent and submit maintenance requests online, enhancing tenant satisfaction and retention.
